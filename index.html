Gun Recommendation For Beginners

The Smith and Wesson XD 9mm

This gun is great for beginners. It's cheaper than a Glock but just of useful and comes with just as many attachments and aftermarket gear. The trigger is something that I'm not extremely excited about but it does get the job done and reset is extremely smooth making rapid fire shooting much easier.

Weight 22.7 oz

Magazine size: 16 rounds

The Mossberg M500 12 gauge shotgun

This gun is especially great for both hunting and for home defense. The butt stock can be inner changeable, after market gear is relatively cheat and easy to come by, and the Weight of the gun is evenly balanced, making those quick shot easier to shoot off.

Weight 5.25 lb

Magazine size: 6 rounds
